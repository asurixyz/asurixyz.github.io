Global Macro Engine

A real-time macroeconomic simulation engine designed to model the complex interplay of monetary policy, fiscal dynamics, and global market shocks. Organized into a unified command terminal, it allows users to assume the role of a central planner, managing critical variables such as inflation, GDP growth, sovereign debt, and currency valuation over a fixed 1200-day term. The simulation features a stochastic event system, dynamic trade relationships, and a reputation-based political stability model, offering a rigorous yet interactive sandbox for exploring the consequences of high-stakes economic decision-making in a volatile global environment.
